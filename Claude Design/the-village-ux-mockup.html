Page-Level Feedback

Reviewed surfaces from frontend/src/pages

Dashboard

Make it intent-led. Combine feed, nearby events, unread replies, active chats, and recommended spaces into one prioritised home.

Spaces and Communities

Unify naming. Use "Spaces" for platform topics/stages and "Groups" for user-created communities. Add privacy badges and join states everywhere.

Forum Post

Improve trust cues: post type, author visibility, professional badge, answer state, report/block controls, and related spaces.

Chats and Messages

Merge the mental model: "Chats" for live rooms, "Messages" for private threads. Add connection rules and clearer empty/offline states.

Events

Bring local events into home and profiles. Add calendar style filters, RSVP reminders, host trust, and online/local grouping.

Stall

Feature-flag honestly. If launched, treat as a marketplace with safety rules, seller trust, location clarity, and message boundaries.

Profile and Settings

Split public profile, account settings, privacy, notifications, and professional verification. Current page is carrying too much.

Saved

Make it a personal library: posts, messages, events, drafts, and resources with filters and reminders rather than only bookmarks.

Admin and Moderator

Keep operational density, but add queues: urgent safety reports, unanswered support posts, professional applications, and community health.

Auth and Onboarding

Remove unavailable social login, or finish it. Onboarding should end with three personalised entry points, not just a dashboard redirect.

Public Pages

Landing, clinicians, terms, privacy, contact, and guidelines should share one public shell and make safety/trust highly visible.

Village+

Clarify what is live versus coming soon. Upgrade moments should appear at limit boundaries, not as broad marketing interruptions.
