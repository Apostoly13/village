# The Village — copywriting bank

Two voices. Use them consistently within each surface — don't mix on one post.

---

## Editorial voice

Quiet, grown-up, literary. Short sentences. Lower-case only in mono metadata.
Italics (Fraunces) carry the emotional word. This is the default for the product,
landing page, and trust-building content.

### Landing
- Eyebrow: `FOR AUSTRALIAN PARENTS · INVITE-ONLY`
- H1: "Parenting was never meant to be done *alone.*"
- Sub: "The Village is a quiet place for Australian parents to talk honestly, find people nearby, and hear from clinicians who actually listen. No ads. No algorithm. No judgement."
- CTA: "Find your village →"
- Proverb block: "Raising a child was never meant to be done alone."

### Landing section headers
- "What's on, near you."
- "Verified clinicians. Real answers."
- "Quiet, honest, yours."
- "Honest pricing. No ads. Ever."

### In-product welcome
- "Welcome to your village."
- "A quieter place to be a parent."

### 3 am Club
- "For the quiet shift."
- "If you're reading this at 3am — you're not alone."

### Verified clinician (pinned reply)
- "Verified midwife reply · pinned"
- Tone: calm, informed, non-diagnostic. "It's a real developmental shift, not something you've done wrong."

## Parent-to-parent voice

Lower-case by default. POV openers. Self-deprecating. Mildly meme-aware. Use
for Reels, meme-style feed posts, DMs from the brand account.

### Reels / openers
- "POV: it's 3am and you're wondering if everyone else is also awake."
- "me at 3am: am I the only person on earth awake rn"
- "stop googling 'is this normal' at 2am"
- "ask a midwife instead. 10/10 recommend"
- "no one tells you parenting is lonely. we did something about it."

### Community / belonging
- "hi. we're 2,400 aus parents and we've got you."
- "your group chat but make it not dying"
- "it's giving… village 🌾"

### Privacy / safety
- "your facebook group read your vent. we won't."
- "post anonymously. be honest anyway."
- "no ads. no algorithm. no 'sponsored by a formula company'."

## Carousel A — 4-month sleep regression, explained (editorial)

1. *The 4-month sleep regression, explained.* · in under 8 swipes.
2. **It's not a regression.** · It's a progression. Sleep cycles are maturing.
3. **What's happening.** · Baby sleep starts to look more like adult sleep — with cycles and brief wake points.
4. **Why it feels brutal.** · They wake between cycles. And they don't yet know how to reconnect them.
5. **How long?** · 2 to 6 weeks. It passes. Honest.
6. **What helps.** · consistent wind-downs · a dark, cool room · holding the boundary · holding each other
7. **What doesn't.** · Comparing to the internet. You are not failing.
8. **You're not alone in this.** · Join 2,400 AU parents at ourlittlevillage.au

## Carousel B — Four privacy promises (editorial)

1. *Four things we promise about your privacy.*
2. **01 · No ads.** Ever. Village+ keeps the lights on — not your data.
3. **02 · Post anonymously.** With one tap. Your name stays yours.
4. **03 · We never sell your story.** Not to brands. Not to researchers. Not to anyone.
5. **04 · You can leave.** And we'll delete everything, for real. No tricks.
